The only Consulting Club in DTU!

Consulting is a hobby that is yet to firmly set foot in DTU. Mostly because of lack of a formal platform, business enthusiasts in our college have been scattered.

With DCG, we seek to cultivate a consulting culture in our college and help our juniors develop business sense that will help them in the long run.

We believe DCG will have a huge impact on people who look forward to work as consultants, and will provide a clear picture and some useful exposure to those who are not sure yet.
